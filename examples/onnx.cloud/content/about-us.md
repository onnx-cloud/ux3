# About Us

Founded in 2023 by members of the ONNX open‑source project, ONNX.cloud
bridges the gap between research and production. Our team consists of
engineers, data scientists and cloud natives who have shipped AI systems
at scale.

We are committed to:

- **Open standards**: all core components are open‑source under the
  Apache 2.0 license.
- **Sustainability**: optimise models for energy efficiency and carbon
  reporting.
- **Community**: collaborate with universities, nonprofits and corporate
  partners.

Headquartered in Berlin with offices in San Francisco and Singapore,
we serve customers worldwide. Learn more about our work on our
[blog](https://onnx.cloud/blog) and check out our
[GitHub repositories](https://github.com/onnx-cloud).
