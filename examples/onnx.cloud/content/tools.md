# Platform Tools

Our toolkit is designed to keep you focussed on the problem, not the
plumbing.

### Fuse

A compiler that converts high‑level model descriptions (ONNX, PyTorch,
TensorFlow) into optimized binaries for CPUs, GPUs and accelerators.
Auto‑reorders operations, applies quantisation and emits
multi‑platform artifacts.

### Freezer

A dataset curation engine that automates cleaning, labelling and
versioning. Connect to your data lake and produce training sets that are
ready for production in minutes rather than weeks.

### Fabric

A messaging and orchestration layer that lets models communicate via a
shared tensor bus. Deploy micro‑services, ensembles or federated
systems with transparent scaling and fault‑tolerance.

Each component is available standalone or as part of the managed
platform. Open‑source clients, CLI tools and SDKs are provided to
integrate with your workflow.
