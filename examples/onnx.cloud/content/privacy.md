# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ONNX.cloud Ltd. and its affiliates
("ONNX.cloud", "we", "us" or "our") collect, use and share personal
information when you visit or use our website (https://onnx.cloud) and
our hosted services (the "Service"). It applies to all visitors,
customers, and other users regardless of location.

## 1. Definitions

- **Personal Information**: any information relating to an identified
  or identifiable natural person.
- **Processing**: any operation performed on Personal Information,
  including collection, storage, use, disclosure or deletion.
- **Controller/Processor**: ONNX.cloud acts as a controller for
  account and marketing data, and a processor for customer data
  uploaded to the platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- Account registration details (name, email, role, company).
- Billing information (address, payment method) collected by our
  payment processor.
- Communications (support tickets, emails, chat transcripts).
- Content and model metadata you upload to the Service.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- Usage data such as API calls, feature usage, error logs.
- Device and browser information (IP address, user agent,
  device identifiers).
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 6).

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- Data from analytics providers (Google Analytics, Plausible).
- Identity verification services for fraud prevention.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use Personal Information for the following purposes:

- To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
- To process transactions and send related information, including
  billing and invoices.
- To respond to your requests, including customer support.
- To personalize your experience and communicate updates.
- To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Legal bases for processing under applicable data protection laws
include consent, performance of a contract, legitimate interests, and
compliance with legal obligations.

## 4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell Personal Information. We may share data in the
following circumstances:

- With service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g.,
  hosting, analytics, payment processing).
- With affiliates and subsidiaries, for internal business purposes.
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with
  notice to affected users).
- To comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, or
  protect rights, property, or safety.

## 5. International Transfers

ONNX.cloud is based in the European Union; data may be transferred and
stored outside your country. When transfers occur, we use appropriate
legal safeguards such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses or rely on
adequacy decisions.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tools for authentication, performance, and
analytics. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings
or opt‑out where available. Third‑party cookies used for analytics may
track your activity across other websites; see their privacy policies
for more information.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain Personal Information for as long as necessary to provide the
Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce
our agreements. Account data is kept for the duration of the account
plus a reasonable period thereafter (generally 2 years) unless you
request deletion earlier.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

- Access to and correction of your Personal Information.
- Deletion or restriction of processing.
- Data portability.
- Objecting to processing or withdrawing consent.

To exercise these rights, contact **privacy@onnx.cloud**. We may need
to verify your identity before acting on requests. We will respond
within the timeframes required by law (typically 30 days).

## 9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical
safeguards to protect Personal Information. This includes encryption
in transit (TLS) and at rest, regular security assessments, data
access controls, and SOC 2 Type II certification. However, no system is
100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

## 10. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 16 (or the applicable
minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect Personal
Information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently
collected such information, please contact us at the email below.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you
of material changes by posting the new policy on our site with an
updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after
changes constitutes your acceptance.
