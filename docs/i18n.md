# i18n — App Guide

Overview
---
App keeps translation files under `examples/iam/ux/i18n/` with per-locale JSON bundles. The compiler aggregates i18n during build and generates type-safe accessors for views.

Where to look
---
- `examples/iam/ux/i18n/en/*.json`
- Generated i18n: `examples/iam/generated/i18n.ts`

Guidelines
---
- Keep keys stable and avoid interpolating large HTML blocks; prefer small strings with placeholders.
- Use translation keys in templates like `{{i18n.actions.RETRY}}`.

Testing
---
- Add snapshot tests for rendered templates with different locales to catch missing keys.

Reference
---
- `src/build/i18n-scanner.ts` (validator tasks may reference it)
